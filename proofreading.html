Sample 1: Academic Paper Proofreading

University Research Paper • English Proofreading

BEFORE EDITING

Original with Errors:

The research was conduct in three different univercity across tehran. Its findings indicate that students whom participate in extracurricular activities have better academic performance. However, there is alot of variables to consider, including socioeconomic status's and parental involvment.

Issues Found:
  • Verb tense error: "was conduct" → "was conducted"
  • Spelling: "univercity" → "university"
  • Capitalization: "tehran" → "Tehran"
  • Grammar: "whom" → "who"
  • Word choice: "alot" → "many"
AFTER EDITING

Corrected Version:

The research was conducted in three different universities across Tehran. The findings indicate that students who participate in extracurricular activities have better academic performance. However, there are many variables to consider, including socioeconomic status and parental involvement.

Improvements Made:
  • Grammar and spelling corrected
  • Academic tone maintained
  • Sentence structure improved
  • Consistent terminology
  • Proper capitalization applied
