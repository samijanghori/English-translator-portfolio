Frequently Asked Questions

Why use audio instead of video for interpretation demo?

Audio focuses purely on interpretation quality. It eliminates visual distractions and emphasizes what truly matters: clarity, accuracy, speed, and tone. Most interpretation work is audio-based (phone, conferences, remote meetings).

How do you handle technical or specialized terminology?

I prepare specialized glossaries for each assignment. For medical cases, I study patient history and terminology. For technical projects, I review documentation in advance to ensure accuracy.

What equipment do I need for remote interpretation?

For remote sessions, you only need a computer with internet access and Zoom/Teams/Skype installed. I handle all technical aspects from my end with professional audio equipment.

Can you provide interpretation for specialized fields?

Yes, I specialize in medical, legal, and technical interpretation. I have extensive glossaries and experience in these fields. I also research specific terminology before each assignment.
